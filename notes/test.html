Pandoc

Pandoc is a powerful commandline tool for converting between document formats.

For example it can:

  • convert from md to pdf
  • convert from md to html (we will discuss this today)
  • convert from docx to md

and much more

Pandoc Header

Pandoc allows us to include a YAML header at the top of the document. This allows us to include metadata such as the title of the document, the author, tags, keywords, and even include-guards.

Include-guards are out of scope but are powerful, capable of changing how pandoc renders parts of the document
